What you can do with Link

  • Fast setup for small groups (camping, events, neighborhoods).
  • Private by design: your group’s network, your rules.
  • Built for durability and low operational overhead.

Quick definitions

Link = the program + onboarding.

Base = the portable kit you bring.

Den = the Denver pilot chapter.

Constraint: We are not building public Wi‑Fi. This is local coordination for groups and neighborhoods.

What it is

A small, bring-your-own network kit (“Base”) plus simple onboarding. It creates local coordination without depending on fragile centralized infrastructure.

What it is not

Not public Wi‑Fi. Not a social app. Not a replacement for your carrier. It’s a local layer that works when the primary layer fails.

Current stage

Pilot + field testing. We’re recruiting early groups to validate use-cases before productizing hardware.
