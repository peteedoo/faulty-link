Den / Eiber — neighborhood pilot node.

A small, local test: group messaging + local info sharing. No hype. Just seeing what works.

How it works Denver Pilot (Den)

Pilot steps (short)

  • Recruit 5–15 households or one organizer-led group.
  • Run 2–3 short tests (30–90 minutes).
  • Collect: friction points, value moments, willingness-to-pay.

What we measure

  • Did people actually use it (not just like it)?
  • Did someone say “we should always bring this”?
  • Would they pay to join early testing?
  • Where did it fail (power, setup, range, clarity)?
